ABOUT US

Welcome to Sidcup Family Golf, where passion meets play! Our state-of-the-art facility offers something for everyone, from beginners to seasoned pros. We combine cutting-edge technology with expert instruction to create an unforgettable golfing experience. Our mission is to make golf accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for players of all ages and skill levels.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Experience golf like never before with our Toptracer Range technology. Track your shots, analyze your game, and compete with friends in various virtual games and challenges. Perfect for players of all skill levels looking to improve their game while having fun.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Embark on an exciting mini-golf adventure through our creatively designed courses. Navigate through challenging obstacles, stunning landscapes, and themed environments that provide entertainment for the whole family. A perfect activity for birthdays, dates, or family outings.

GOLF LESSONS

Take your game to the next level with our professional golf lessons. Our experienced instructors provide personalized coaching tailored to your skill level and goals. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your technique, our lessons will help you improve consistently.
